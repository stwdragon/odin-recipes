Thai Curry

Delicious Bowl of Thai Green Curry

Thai Green Curry is a delicious soup that is both sweet and salty while packing a spicy punch. It can be made whichever meat and vegetable you like, but here we will be using chicken along with bamboo shoots, green and red bell pepper, basil, and baby corn. The base is made from rich and creamy coconut milk and makes this dish sure to please all.

Ingredients

  • 1 can Green Curry Paste
  • 1 can Coconut Milk
  • 1 lb Chicken
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 small can Bamboo Shoots
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Brown the chicken in a pan with a small amount of oil
  2. Just before Chicken is fully cooked fry the curry paste for 30 seconds
  3. Add in the cocnut milk to the curry paste and incorporate
  4. Let the mixture cook for 5 minutes at medium heat
  5. Add in the chopped vegetables and bamboo shoots, reduce to low heat and cover
  6. Serve with steamed rice and enjoy!
